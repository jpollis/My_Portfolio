After spending the first 10 years of my professional career in sales & business, I decided I wanted to do something that I'm passionate about. A developer friend suggested I try coding. So that night I signed up for a free online course, and haven't looked back since. That night, when I solved my first difficult challenge, I knew that this is what I wanted to do in life. So I decided to attend Wyncode Academy, and turn my passion project into my career.

The technologies that I use on a daily basis are: Ruby, Rails, Javascript, jQuery, HTML/CSS, Materialize/Bootstrap, Git, Github, Trello, and many more. Some of the concepts that I practice are: Object Oriented Programming, Functional Programming, MVC, TDD, and agile methodologies.

I am excited to continue learning and growing with this passion of mine, and become a well rounded developer. My goal is to enjoy what I do and never stop learning!
